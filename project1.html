rubber match

Thesis project at Emily Carr University of Art + Design, class of 2020.

A modular synthesizer with no labels on inputs, outputs, switches, or knobs. Users navigate the interface in a feedback-driven process of exploration, collaboration, and music-making.

Inspired by the works of Byung-Chul Han and Donna Haraway.
